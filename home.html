Design to Race

Who are we?

We are Ruhuna Racing, a team of young and innovative undergraduates representing the Faculty of Engineering, University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka. We design and fabricate an open-wheeled Formula Student electric racing car afresh with the aim of competing in the Formula Student competitions.

Formula Student is an educational student engineering competition, run by the Institution of Mechanical Engineers, in which student teams from across the globe build a single seated formula style car and compete with each other.
